Culture & Strengths

We have a culture that inspires a passion for science and innovation. What we do is at the heart of who we are as an organisation. The nature of our business makes high levels of  collaboration and cross functional engagements almost impossible to live without. We have an open culture that encourages employees to share their suggestions and ideas related what we do , or how we do it.

We are a business of the future more than the now, and that is reflected in the upbeat, optimistic, collegiate environment at the office. We still have remnants of our start up tag in our culture, which means almost everyone practices boundary less multitasking and people are always on the look out for new areas to contribute to. Our average age is in the mid thirties, and therefore young, energetic and dynamic goes without saying.
