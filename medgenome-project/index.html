Figure 2 : Validation of WGS/WES pipelines using Gold Standard datasets

  • Sensitivity for SNV detection of our pipeline exceeded 99% at 30X-50X coverage and was over 99% for the detection of Indels at 50X coverage.

  • The NIST dataset provided the best sensitivity and precision primarily due to higher sequencing depth (~50X) and PCR-free library.

  • Our study on NA12878 gold standard results shows that our results are at par with industry standard and matches FDA challenge results.

We have recently added the 10X Chromium platform to our offerings and can perform single-cell Whole Genome Sequencing , and analysis as well as de-novo analysis using the 10X software solutions.
